About Us


The Sheringham Portal Project is a non-profit initiative that has been founded with the intention of creating a phone application that will allow users to interact with the vibrant history of our town.

Our goal is to create a 360* virtual tour of 'old' Sheringham using archived photos from the 1940's and 50's.

There will be many opportunities for local people to get involved, we will also be working closely with local businesses in the hope of encouraging postitive tourism.

Our Goals




1) Use innovative methods to preserve the unique history of our coastal town.
2) Boost positive tourism and bring a modern exciting service to Sheringham.
3) Encourage local youths to be proud of their roots and to engage with their history.





Sheringham has such a proud and vibrant history, however in such a fast evolving world our traditions and values are sadly at risk of being forgotten, I strongly believe their are many others who realise how much of a shame this would be.

We want to utilise technology in a positive way, in order to bring people together rather than creating divides.

Our mission is to use the latest artificial Intelligence technologies in order to create a snapshot in time of Sheringham, allowing the past and future to come together in perfect harmony.
